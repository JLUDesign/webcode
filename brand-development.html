Brand DNA

Purpose (why)
We see from customers that they are in need for more "care” when it comes to legal advice. Therefore, we believe that receiving legal advice should be as caring as going to your local GP (General Practitioner/ Family Doctor).
‍
Positioning (what)
Yuppen that strive for success, between 25 and 35 years old, new to work, not familiar with the law, in need for clear process and transparency.
‍
Personality (how)
Genuine, smart, reliable, friendly, neat and go-getter.Positioning statement

Positioning
statement

According to these elements of our analysis and Brand DNA, we came up with the following positioning statement.
‍
“For yuppen, Blauwslag offers conﬂict resolutions that are accessible, provided by legal experts and gives people the support and conﬁdence to be successful.”
‍
For the competition axis we have selected expert and novice as the ﬁrst axis. This was chosen because SRK (the mother company of Blauwslag )has an array of experienced lawyers and that is a point of distinction. The second axis we have considered is sympathetic and distant. This was selected in line with caring which we have seen through our 4C analysis and is line with GP.

Rationale

Coherence

The purpose of our brand highlights the insights from the interviews we had conducted. We have used our positioning and purpose to reinforce this, by focusing on the latent needs of our target group such as their go-getter attitude.

Relevance 

The cultural trend we have based our brand upon is security which is twofold. The ﬁrst is digital security of sensitive information and the second is the sense of security when facing uncertainty.

Credibility

The main product is a service provided through a website. We have highlighted the concerns consumers face when accessing these digital services, like lacking trustworthiness and reliability. To relieve the concerns, The ﬁnal brand is highlighting two major points in the product and service. The ﬁrst is the care which would be delivered in the interaction of the digital services and the second is the security feeling which could be sensed when interacting with the professional lawyers.

Logo

The logo and the mono logo are on the right. The mono logo is only used when the primary logo is not visible due to the colour of background.

Colour palette 

Solid colours are selected for our colour palette to portray conﬁdence.

Typography

‘Avenir LT Std’ was combined with serif font ‘Liberation Serif’ to create a tension ﬁeld between our characteristics reliable / smart and go-getter / friendly.

Photography

We believe photography must be used to convey care. This allows people to have the associations of a supportive but professional environment that Blauwslag wants to provide.
‍
The imagery conveys a nice balance between staged and authentic scenes. The photography ﬁlters used add a warm ambience to the images. The lighting is bright to give it fresh and go-getter feel.

Advertisement

Our advertisement shows imagery of yuppen enjoying their free-time, which they work hard for during the week.

Launch strategy

The launch campaign has been targeted towards the early adopters, young urban professionals (yuppen) in the Netherlands. The launch strategy has two goals, the ﬁrst one is to create awareness and the second is to build traction among the early adopters to use Blauwslag.
