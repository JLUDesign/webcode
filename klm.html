WISDOM
OF
THE CROWD

Through barriers of hierarchy, a lack of feedback loops and utilisation of people’s passion and knowledge, KLM employees do not feel driven enough to actively partake in innovation within KLM. Departments are detached and working towards differing goals which results in concepts being created and not implemented - wasting valuable resources. The goal of this project is to improve the cross-departments communication, provoke the innovative atmosphere of the company and make the employees feel be valued.

VISION

The vision of this project is to create a working environment which harnesses the passion, experience and knowledge of all employees to achieve the common goal of KLM - through breaking the barriers of hierarchy and allowing employees to actively participate in innovative processes. It is to ensure more transparency and congruence in involving the right people at the right time in the right way.

It would be an environment which harnesses the collective wisdom of KLM - one which promotes creative interaction, where all employees are a part of the implementation as well as the creation of innovation within KLM. A system that breaks down the barriers of hierarchy and creates a human being centric company. A company that uses calm technology to communicate the necessary information at the right time - combating data overload by aiding employee’s work instead of adding to it.

WORK PROCESS

Wanting to work with a method that is proving successful within parts of KLM, a change to the 6-step structure was proposed. This is used within parts of KLM-X and would prove to be successful in a fast developing company if it is altered. Additional steps called "OWL", "SCULPTURE" and "CINEMA" are used to harness the collective wisdom of KLM.

OWL - the collective wisdom of KLM, where the only iterative cycle is needed when refining ideas. It starts with defining the concept, determining aspects of relevance, and having an interactive cycle of idea creation. This stage includes all members of KLM. Further stages include refining of ideas, and sculpting the aesthetics or details of the implementation, which is completed by KLMX. The final stage is providing feedback through the chosen ambassadors, to allow employees to see that their ideas are being used and prepare them for implementation. This also helps employees know that KLM is constantly innovating, and is a company worth being proud of working for.

With the participation of all employees, ideas are implemented faster. Ideas become more refined based on more input. Ideas are also more positively and efficiently received as employees are a part of the construction phases. Due to a sense of ownership, there will also be more support of changes, and an overall understanding throughout all departments. Rather than a team passing an idea onto different implementers, ideas are created by KLM, for KLM.
